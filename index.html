Minecreeper

The town is under a terrible curse.

When the full moon's sickly light illuminates the local graveyard, it causes the restless undead to stir in their graves and thirst for blood!

Click to reveal each square, but take care not to disturb the undead. Right-click to mark their locations with tombstones and send them back to eternal slumber.

Your inner eye tells you how many undead lurk next to each square. Good luck out there...
